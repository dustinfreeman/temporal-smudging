“I want a frame where I’m looking at the pedestal and it’s empty”
Old-school photoshopping does this by feathering edges.
How To Feather in GIMP
  1. right click your layer
  2. alpha to selection
  3. Select: shrink 8
  4. Select: feather 8
  5. Select: invert
  6. Edit: Clear
You now have a selected layer with feathered edges
